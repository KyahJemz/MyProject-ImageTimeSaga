 Description:
* Each level has three images linked to a one word.  The player's goal is to identify the word from the letters provided below the images.

How to Play:
* Choose a letter by clicking it from the choices below to form a word related to the images given.
* Clicking a letter from the answer box will take that letter back to choices box.

Controls:
* Back button - To return to menu.
* Shuffle Button - To shffle the choices below.
* Clear Button - To clear the answer box.
